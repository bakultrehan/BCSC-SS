Why you would use this application

The BC Services Card Self Service application enables you as team member of a BPS agency to have an easy -access point into deploying the BC Services Card as an authentication credential for the your BPS' web application.

We use a two-stepped approach where BPS teams can prototype an integration in our test environment, using fictitious BC Services Card data as a test and, when they are ready for production (go live), prepare and submit their materials to integrate the production BC Services Card system. Please use the Self Assessment below to see if this is a fit for your organization.

Self Assessment:

Are you:

  1. looking to provide high identity assurance the BC Services Card provides for your?

  2. an employee in one of the British Columbia’s Broader Public Sector agencies?

  3. part of an identity and authentication services team with experience in Open ID Connect (OIDC) Protocols

If you’ve answered yes to all the questions, we look forward to working with you. Let’s get started.

If you answered, “no” to any of the questions above, we recommend requesting a consultation through our contact us page. to initiate a conversation around your identity and authentication needs .
