Why you would use this application

The BC Services Card Self Service application enables you as team member of a BPS agency to have an easy -access point into deploying the BC Services Card as an authentication mechanism for the your BPS' web application.

We use a two-stepped approach where BPS teams can experiment with an integration in our test environment, using fictitious BC Services Card data as a test and, when they are ready, prepare and submit their materials to create a live integration with real citizen BC Services Cards. Please use the Self Assessment below to see if this is a fit for your organization.

Self Assessment:

Are you:

  1. an employee in one of the British Columbia’s Broader Public Sector agencies?

  2. wanting to use digital technologies that are transforming the way British Columbians live, work and play?

  3. part of a team that has experience in identity and authentication services for online applications?

  4. part of a team that understands (or is willing to learn) about Open ID Connect (OIDC) protocols?

  5. looking to leverage the high identity assurance the BC Services Card provides?

If you’ve answered yes to all the questions, we look forward to working with you. Let’s get started.

If you answered no to any of the questions above, we recommend requesting a consultation with IDIM.Consulting to better understand your needs as the BC Services Card Self Service application may not be suitable for your organization.
