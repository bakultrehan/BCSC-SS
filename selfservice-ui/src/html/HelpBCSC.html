About the BC Services Card

 

The BC Services Card provides individuals with access to government services and can be paired with your mobile device to enable online access to participating web-based services.

Our self-serve application allows your organization to easily integrate your web-based services with the BC Services Card authentication method. As the BC Services Card has a Level 3 assurance rating, which provides you with a high level of confidence that a user is who they claim to be.
